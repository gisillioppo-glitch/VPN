Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

What ORBIT Is

ORBIT is a private beta VPN service built on self-hosted Outline infrastructure. This policy explains what we collect during the beta and how we use it.

What We Collect

  • Name, email, selected plan, account status, and request date.
  • Outline access key ID, key name, and key status for support and revocation.
  • Basic operational server data needed to keep the service running.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not intentionally log browsing history.
  • We do not sell customer data.
  • We do not use access keys as public links or public profile pages.

How Data Is Used

We use customer records to approve beta access, create or revoke Outline keys, provide support, prevent abuse, and communicate service updates.

Beta Limits

ORBIT is in private beta. Features, pricing, infrastructure, and availability may change while we validate stability and demand.

Contact

For privacy questions or key resets, contact hello@orbitvpn.co.
